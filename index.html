A little astrology

Long ago when I was young and full of curiosity, I stumbled across a book that would change my life. It was "Love Signs" by Linda Goodman and it had helped me get through a VERY tough breakup by showing me the insights to each of our signs.

Since then, I devoured all the books I could find about astrology. Then I began to write a summary of each sign so I can refrence it when needed. When people found out I had these summaries, they would ask me for them constantly. Now I am sharing them with the world.
